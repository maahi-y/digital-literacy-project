What this is about
This is a collection of tasks I did as part of the Digital Literacy course at VIT Bhopal. Honestly I expected it to be mostly theory, but it was way more hands-on. It pushed me to actually set up my GitHub, LinkedIn and Kaggle profiles which I kept putting off.

What the project covers
What digital literacy actually means in day to day life
Setting up and cleaning up my profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn and Kaggle
Do's and Don'ts of staying safe online
A UPI fraud case study — how it works and how people fall for it
A basic cyber safety checklist
Quiz questions to test what I learned (MCQ + short answer)

Something I found useful
The UPI fraud case study was the most interesting part for me. I knew scams existed but never really understood how they work step by step until I went through it properly.
The one thing that stuck — your UPI PIN is only for sending money. Nobody needs your PIN to send money to you. If someone asks for it, just don't.
